### **Forget-Me-Not Bag Paper Prototype**
This is my paper prototype of the Forget-Me-Not Bag. I changed it to have two pockets. Each pocket has a circut connecting an LED and a "homemade button" to a pin on the LilyPad. When the circuts are closed meaning the lights are on, and the button is pressed a sound will play!\
